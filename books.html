Using the Advanced Search

The search bar operates as a powerful query engine. It evaluates your search terms logically to filter the exact books you want.

  • Keywords: Typing a regular word searches titles and authors.
  • Exact Phrases: Wrap words in quotes like "time loop" to search titles, authors, and synopses for that exact string.
  • Specific Fields: Prefix your search with tag: or author: (e.g., tag:fantasy).
  • Logic Operators: Use and, or, and not to combine terms.
  • Parentheses: Group conditions together like in math ().

Example Query:
"wind" and (not author:rothfuss) or tag:litrpg
